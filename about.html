About Engineering Physics Lab Suite

This comprehensive web-based toolkit provides nine essential physics lab calculators used in engineering and applied physics curricula. Each module follows standard experimental formulas and delivers real-time results.

Available Modules:

  • Resistivity (Four-Probe) - Semiconductor analysis and sheet resistance
  • Bragg's Law - X-ray diffraction d-spacing determination
  • Young's Modulus - Beam bending method for elastic modulus
  • Photon Energy - Planck-Einstein relation (eV & Joules)
  • Newton's Rings - Radius of curvature of a lens
  • Hall Effect - Carrier concentration & Hall coefficient
  • Dielectric Constant - Relative permittivity measurement
